About

We at Codifica have traveled far and wide to search for the best coffee shops around the world. From the concrete jungle of New York City to the tech-craved, disruptive haven of San Francisco, our team has committed countless hours into researching what makes a coffee shop, well, a coffee shop.

The purpose of our research is to make Codifica a coffee experience unlike any other, by combining the best attributes and features of coffee repos around the world.
