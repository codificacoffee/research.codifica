Research


Good artists borrow, great artists steal.”

Following the words of Picasso himself, we at Codifica have traveled far and wide to search for the best coffee shops around the world. From the concrete jungle of New York City to the tech-craved, disruptive haven of San Francisco, our team has committed countless hours into researching what makes a coffee shop, well, a coffee shop.

The purpose of our research is to make Codifica a coffee experience unlike any other, by combining the best attributes and features of coffee repos around the world.

Events

Open Source Night

A night of pure open-source contributions, held every other Tuesday of the month. Invite your friends, improve existing software.

Fork It Ship It

One of our more high energy events, held every other Thursday of the month. The goal is to ship code by the end of the night.

Open Brew

An install fest that aims to familiarize users with various package managers - pip, gem, npm, and apt-get. Held on the first Monday of every month.
